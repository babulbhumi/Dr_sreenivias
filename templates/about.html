Aesthetic Medicine & PMU

Dr. Veli Velli Jaswantha Lakshmi

MBBS · Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine · Certified PMU Artist

Registered: APMC / FMR / 104005

Dr. Veli Velli Jaswantha Lakshmi is a specialist in aesthetic medicine who combines rigorous clinical training with an artist's precision. Registered under the Andhra Pradesh Medical Council (APMC / FMR / 104005), she brings a dual perspective to every treatment — the accuracy of a physician and the sensitivity of a trained PMU artist. She is celebrated for her mastery of injectables, non-surgical rejuvenation, and permanent makeup techniques that look natural and last beautifully. Patients value her empathetic, unhurried approach and her commitment to delivering exactly what she promises.
